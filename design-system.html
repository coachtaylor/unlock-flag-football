Rules
01
Sentence case everywhere.Never Title Case. ALL CAPS only at 11px micro-labels.
02
No em dashes.Use commas, periods, or rewrite the sentence.
03
American English."Color," not "colour." "Practice," not "practise."
04
Mobile headings never exceed two lines.Rewrite shorter, don't shrink type.
05
Numbers use mono with tabular figures.Times, scores, counts, deltas — never sans.
06
Letter-spacing is tight at display, loose at micro.−0.4 to −0.8 at 28px+, +0.5 at 11px.

06Motion

Motion serves feedback, not decoration. Press states are 80ms scale-down to 0.97. Section reveals stay under 350ms. If a loader spins for more than a second, you should have shown a skeleton instead.

08Hard rules

The non-negotiables. Anything outside these is a system-level decision, not a screen-level one.

01
Dark mode only.surface.base everywhere. No light mode in scope.
02
Orange has one job.Interactive: CTAs, selected states, brand moment. Never decorative.
03
Sentence case.ALL CAPS only at 11px micro-labels.
04
Touch targets ≥ 44 × 44.Use hitSlop to extend if visual size is smaller.
05
20px screen horizontal padding, single column.Mobile is the primary surface.
06
Every card gets a border.border-subtle minimum. No borderless floating cards on dark.
07
Locked insight copy: "Add X to unlock Y."Never guilt. Never gate behind dark patterns.
08
All tags in a group share the selected color.Orange. No exceptions.
09
Numbers always mono with tabular figures.Times, scores, counts, deltas.
10
Varied vertical rhythm.32px between sections, 16px between cards, 12px inside. Uniform = flat.

09Anti-patterns

If you're tempted to do any of these, the answer is a different solution at the system level, not a workaround at the screen level.

×Orange used decoratively or as a tint
×Gradients on brand surfaces (the card bloom is the only allowed one)
×A third type weight beyond what's defined
×ALL CAPS outside 11px micro-labels
×Em dashes in any drafted copy
×Stock photography of generic athletes
×Fake testimonials, social proof, or logo walls
×Borderless floating cards on dark surfaces
×Filled and outline icons mixed on one screen
×Emoji in production UI or marketing copy
×Pure #000 backgrounds or #FFF text
×Sans-serif numbers on stats or scoreboards
